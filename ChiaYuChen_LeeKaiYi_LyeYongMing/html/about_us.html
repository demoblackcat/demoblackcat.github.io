About Us

Restaurant History and Background

Our restaurant was founded in 1985 by Mr.Chia , who came from a long line of Chinese chefs.
He opened our restaurant with a vision to share the authentic flavors of his hometown with the local community.

Over the years, we have become a beloved destination for diners seeking high-quality,
delicious Chinese cuisine. Our restaurant has won numerous awards and accolades, and we continue
to strive for excellence in everything we do.
